AbuseIPDB is a collaborative project where users report abusive IP addresses engaged in hacking, spamming, and other malicious activity.

This feed contains IPs with approximately 100% abuse confidence score that were reported within the last 30 days. The data is aggregated by borestad from the AbuseIPDB database.

A 100% confidence score means that essentially all reports for these IPs indicate abusive behavior. This is the broader variant covering a full month of reports, providing wider coverage but potentially including IPs that are no longer active threats.

See also: abuseipdb_1d for IPs reported in the last 1 day only.
